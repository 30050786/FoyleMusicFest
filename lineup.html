Summer 2026 - Line-Up

Welcoming artists from UK/Ireland and some international stars too!

EsDeeKid

UK Rapper

EsDeeKid is an English rapper from Liverpool. He was named one of the years breakout artists in 2024. Best known for his Rebel album.

KNEECAP

Irish Rap/EDM

Kneecap are an Irish hip hop trio from Belfast, Ireland, composed of Mo Chara, Móglaí Bap, and DJ Próvaí. Known for their political messages.

KETTEMA

House/Techno DJ

Evan Campbell, known professionally as Kettama, is an Irish electronic music producer and DJ.
Age: 28

HOLY PRIEST

Hard Techno/Uptempo DJ

Holy Priest is a hard techno DJ from Germany. Well-known and easily recognised with his signature mask with a cross.

blk.

Electronic Techno DJ

blk. – Artistsblk. is an Irish hard techno artist known for his high-energy performances, successful global tours, and tracks with millions of plays.
